Gain Visibility & Scale Operations with a Freelancer Management System

Time and budget resources can go to waste without a systematic approach to engaging independent talent. Private Talent Cloud delivers a comprehensive freelancer management system that scales from hiring one person to building a team of thousands. Plus, it provides your team visibility and control over your company’s existing SOW spend, which often escapes traditional systems.

Private Talent Cloud creates a closed network of your company’s entire independent workforce. The platform has built-in tools to seamlessly onboard and pay them efficiently. In need of fresh talent? Tap into a global freelancer network of specialized talent to invite new members into your company’s Private Talent Cloud. Plus, the Private Talent Cloud team can provide worker classification services to ensure your freelancers fully comply with local employment and labor laws.
