PARTNER COVERAGE TO MATCH YOUR MARKET

How many companies or perspective clients in your target market?
How big your partner network should be and many partners you need starts by understanding the size of your total available market.

While it depends on your market, the margin economics of your business, the lifetime value of your clients and the economics of your partner's business model, typically services and Cloud/SAAS technology target a partner coverage ratio between 1:5 and 1:100. Ask yourself how many prospects or clients could a partner realistically touch in a given year. What is the realistic yield of a partner's efforts and what impact will it have on your business?
